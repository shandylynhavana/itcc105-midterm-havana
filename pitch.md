# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot

## Problem Statement
The old student portal is inefficient because it relies on too many menus and buttons. Students often get confused navigating different sections just to perform simple tasks like checking grades, clearance, or announcements. This results in wasted time, frustration, and poor user experience.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot empowers students by assisting them rather than replacing them. Instead of forcing users to learn the system, the AI adapts to the user’s needs through natural language or voice commands. This creates a more human-centered system where technology enhances productivity, decision-making, and accessibility.

## Agile Requirements (User Stories)

1. As a student, I want the AI to check my clearance status, so that I can quickly know if I am eligible for enrollment.

2. As a student, I want the AI to show my schedule instantly, so that I can manage my time better.

3. As a student, I want the AI to answer campus-related questions, so that I don’t need to navigate multiple pages.
