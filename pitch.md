# ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot

## Problem Statement
The old student portal is inefficient because it depends on too many menus and manual navigation. Students struggle to quickly find important information like schedules, announcements, and grades. This leads to confusion, delays, and poor user experience.

## Industry 5.0 Justification
The ASSCAT Campus AI Copilot empowers students by making technology more human-centered. Instead of navigating complex menus, students can simply ask questions using chat or voice. The AI supports students by providing instant answers, improving productivity without replacing human decision-making.

## Agile Requirements (User Stories)

1. As a student, I want the AI to show my class schedule, so that I can manage my time easily.

2. As a student, I want the AI to display latest announcements, so that I stay updated with campus events.

3. As a student, I want the AI to check my grades, so that I can monitor my academic performance.
